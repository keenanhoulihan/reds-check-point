Our Story

93 Years on the South Side

Red's Check Point has stood at the corner of this neighborhood since 1932 — through the Depression, through the postwar boom, through every change Chicago's South Side has seen. We've outlasted trends, chains, and competition because we've always done one thing: treated every customer the way we'd treat family.

Today, owner George Franklin carries that same standard forward. When you bring your car here, you deal with us directly. No commission-chasing service writers. No calls to some corporate hotline. You talk to the people doing the work, and we give you the truth about what your vehicle needs.

West Pullman is our neighborhood too. We live here, our kids go to school here, and we depend on the same roads you do. That's not a marketing line — it's why we take every repair personally.
